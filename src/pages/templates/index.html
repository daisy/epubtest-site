Welcome to epubtest.org, the home for accessibility testing of e-reading experiences. This site can be used to find out how an e-reader performs with different features found in eBooks. The data here is continually collected from a large group of expert testers from all over the globe who volunteer their time, and testing is coordinated by the DAISY Consortium and is open to participation.
